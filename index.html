P2P Private Messaging

The only person with your private key is you. When you create an account, your private key and ability to decrypt messages never leaves your device. We utilize IPFS through our Warp project to allow you to connect directly with your friends, and control your own keys.

File Sharing

Share files of any size, direct with your contacts and groups. Warp will encrypt, shard (break apart), and pin your files to IPFS, and allow you to share the file with anyone you want. Data is currently stored locally, with future options to store in the cloud.

Multi-Stream Voice/Video Calling (Coming soon)

Call your friends, directly. Once you establish a connection, the only limit to your stream quality is your own network connection. We are experimenting with codecs to reduce the stream size as much as possible.

Extensions (Coming Soon)

Build your own, or use other peoples extensions to customize and make Uplink work the way you want. Documentation coming soon.
