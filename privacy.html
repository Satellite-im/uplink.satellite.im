Privacy Policy

Last updated: 04/18/2024

We don't track our users, and we don't sell any data.

Satellite does not collect any user data. All data is sent encrypted and is un-usable to any party other than the intended recipient. Thus, Satellite does not collect, sell, distribute or track any user data. Satellite tracks anonymous data such as download counts and page views without identifying user information.

Satellite cannot garuntee the same privacy policy exists in 3rd party services and libraries we use, however we do our best to avoid using services which track our users.
